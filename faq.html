💼 Applying for Jobs

How do I apply for a job?
You can browse our job listings on the Jobs page and apply directly through our website by submitting your CV and cover letter.
Can I apply for multiple jobs at once?
Yes! You can apply for multiple job openings that match your qualifications and interests at the same time.
How long does the hiring process take?
The hiring process varies depending on the position and employer. Typically, it takes between 2 to 4 weeks from application to a final decision.
Do you provide CV and resume-building assistance?
Yes! We offer CV-building workshops and personalised feedback to help strengthen your job application and stand out to employers.

📚 Courses & Training

What courses does Hot Beans offer?
We offer a range of web development courses covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more. Visit our Courses page to see the full list.
Are the training programs free?
Some of our training programs are free, while others require a fee. Please check the individual course details on the Courses page for pricing information.
Do I need any experience to join a course?
No prior experience is needed for our beginner courses. We welcome people from all backgrounds — whether you are completely new to coding or looking to build on existing skills.
Will I receive a certificate after completing a course?
Yes, upon successful completion of a course you will receive a certificate that you can add to your CV and professional profiles.
